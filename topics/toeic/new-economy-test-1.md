> A. I would really appreciate the opportunity to work with you.

> A. at  
> B. to ✅   
> C. with

- recently: gần đây  
- came across: tình cờ  
We came across each other yesterday.
- appreciate: trân trọng, cảm kích, đánh giá cao  
I appreciate your help.
- in fact: trên thực tế  
He looks lazy. In fact, he is very hardworking.
- illustrate: minh họa  
You could illustrate the above sentence for us.
- specialize in: chuyên về
- look forward to: mong đợi, trông chờ
